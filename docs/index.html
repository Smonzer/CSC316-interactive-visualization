Tourism Arrivals 1995–2020

Explore international tourist arrivals by country and region (World Bank WDI: ST.INT.ARVL)

How to use this interactive visualization
  • Filter by Region: Use the Region dropdown to view specific geographic areas, or click legend items
  • Filter by Country: Select individual countries to compare their tourism trends
  • Change Measure: Switch between Total Arrivals, Year-over-Year Growth, Growth Rate, or Normalized view
  • Time Range: Adjust the year sliders to focus on specific periods
  • Animate: Click the Play button to watch tourism evolve year by year
  • Hover: Mouse over any line to see detailed statistics and percentage changes
  • Focus: Click any line to highlight it and dim others for comparison
  • Zoom: Scroll to zoom in/out on the timeline; drag to pan
  • Reset: Double-click the chart or use the Reset button to restore defaults
Data Source: World Bank WDI — International tourism, number of arrivals (1995–2020) (License: CC BY 4.0)
